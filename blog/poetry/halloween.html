Halloween

On Halloween -

Can't tell

If I'm a hell-

raiser or sent

from heaven -

I must be something in between

Here on earth / our words

Say only one thing more than who we are

I try to outthink the universe

try to rhyme my final verse

Figure out if I'm to be / royalty

Or sent back / for lack / of humility

Here on earth / our births

are when we learn

That we are going to die

And say: not I -,

Jealous of the living.

Tyler  Brown
Cifu Shuster
