New Subjects

New subjects, late night time differences, jumping at ringtones,

Subtle smirks and soporific SMSs

Waiting & planning, overhead for possibilities planned and could,

Long walks full of thoughts and hands empty of hands

Stares, recommendations, simultaneous laughs that never see each other

And cold hopeful nights limited by probability
