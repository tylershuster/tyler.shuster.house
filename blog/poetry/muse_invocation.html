Muse Invocation

Muse I greet you with a mythy head full of not-falses,

May you turn them into truths: songs & waltzes

Bless the lines I write today,

keep my stumbling self at bay

Hip-hop poetry in obscenity

produced by urban verbal prodigies

procured form mediocrity

and thrust!

upon the waking earth.

Give me a line - give me a verse

something to stir the cold -

stagnant and perverse.

Tyler  Brown
Cifu Shuster
