Faith and Future

With terror and trembling I take her hand

Where faith and future meet

And in the silent stillness of sorrow

Wise words from wondrous holy seat

Meet us marvelously and murmur

"be bold, my brave ones, be bold,

And take this truth through these trials;

Love long lost will light again,

And charity's child will choose a chance,

To help these hearts where there has been hurt

And heal those holes of harm and hate,

To guide the Godless to greater good

And verily vindicate the righteous vexed."

Tyler  Brown
Cifu Shuster
