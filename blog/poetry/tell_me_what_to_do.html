Tell Me What to Do

Tell me what to do & I will do it.

Tell me how to be & I will depend on you.

Write me a letter & I will read every word.

Ask me a question & I will be completely stupified.

I know nothing you do not.

I can only agree with myself.

Tell me what to throw away,

Tell me I always am.

Write me of your love.

Ask me questions whose answers you know.

We will sort ourselves out in time.

We will sort ourselves out of time.

Tyler  Brown
Cifu Shuster
